As a non-profit organization, we rely on the unique skills and talents of our volunteers. We need people who are willing to devote their time and energy to making positive change in their own lives and in the lives of others in the community.

Current Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Campus Ambassador
  • Graphic designer
  • Research assistant
  • Clerical or administration assistance
  • Internet marketing
  • Video creation
  • Fundraising
  • Rehab Worker
  • Social media coordinator

Please fill out the form below if you would like to volunteer with us. Please specify in which areas you may be interested in volunteering, as well as your rough availability. If you have any questions or concerns you can contact us by phone at 604-779-2472 or email volunteer@vbia.ca.
