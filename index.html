Spread The Message, Help is Available!

Even during these trying times, help is available for people living with acquired brain injury.

      


Living with a brain injury can make daily life tremendously difficult, confusing, and frustrating. Especially during COVID-19, these challenges are becoming too hard to bear for many. However, we at VBIA bring direct services to people with acquired brain injury and their caregivers, including access to related counselling, information, and support group programs.

Please email help@vbia.ca for brain recovery or advice.

What is Giving Tuesday Now?

#GivingTuesdayNow is a global day of giving and unity that will take place on May 5, 2020 as an emergency response to the unprecedented need caused by COVID-19. This is an opportunity for people, in Canada, and around the world to stand together in unity - to use their individual power of generosity to remain connected and heal.

We invite all Canadians to join us in demonstrating the power of generosity by contributing charitable donations. By participating in #GivingTuesdayNow, you can spread awareness and give back to charities and nonprofits like Vancouver Brain Injury Association.
